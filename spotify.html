Spotify Cover Playlists

Ever since I started using Spotify back in Grade 9, I found Spotify’s own playlist covers to be very simple yet eye-catching. With those covers as inspiration, I started creating covers for my own playlists using Adobe Photoshop. Designing my own covers was my creative outlet throughout highschool.

Below is an example of how my design has changed over the years. The names of my playlists may have changed over time.

2017 K-Pop Cover

2020 K-Pop Cover

As you can see above, the 2017 playlist cover’s text is illegible due to the distracting picture behind it. I may have had too much fun over-saturating pictures back then. With this in mind, every time I redesign covers now, I would choose a simple, neat picture such as the one in the 2020 cover. This picture has one main colour, purple, which makes it easier to look at than the previous one. I shifted the picture over a bit in order to make some space for the text on the left. In this way, the two elements don’t clash like they did in the 2017 cover. I have also realized over time that increasing the picture's contrast and decreasing its brightness results in better readability of the white text.
