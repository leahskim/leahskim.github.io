Adobe Illustrator

I started learning how to use Adobe Illustrator this past April by following tutorials on YouTube. However, as somebody who is not that great at drawing in real life, vector art was quite an eye-opener for me. I enjoyed finding design inspirations on Dribbble, Designspiration, as well as colour palette ideas from COLOURLovers. I eventually started making my own illustrations for fun by experimenting with such designs and colours.

The illustration above is the same illustration you saw on the previous page. The colour I used for the laptop is the same pink I have been using throughout the website (#FF9C9A). After fine-tuning a colour palette that a site generated for me, the colour palette above was complete.
